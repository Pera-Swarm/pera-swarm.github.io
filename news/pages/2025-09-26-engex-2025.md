<p>The Department of Computer Engineering, University of Peradeniya, is proud to present Pera-Swarm, an innovative research project, at&nbsp;<strong>EngEx 2025</strong>, the grand exhibition celebrating the 75th Anniversary of the Faculty of Engineering.</p>
<p>Visitors can experience Pera-Swarm at the&nbsp;<strong>&ldquo;Smart City by PeraCom&rdquo;</strong>&nbsp;stall during the exhibition, which will run from September&nbsp;<strong>23 to 28, 2025, at the University of Peradeniya</strong>. The showcase will feature:</p>
<ul>
<li>Live demonstrations of coordinated robot behaviors</li>
<li>Mixed reality visual overlays for situational awareness</li>
<li>Interactive control and monitoring dashboards</li>
<li>Insights into research on autonomy, communication, and safety</li>
</ul>
<p>This exhibition aims to inspire students, researchers, and industry partners to engage with next-generation cyber-physical systems. Team members will be available to discuss technical architecture, deployment frameworks, and opportunities for collaboration.</p>
<p>Be sure to stop by the&nbsp;<strong>&ldquo;Smart City by PeraCom&rdquo;</strong>&nbsp;stall at&nbsp;<strong>EngEx 2025</strong>&nbsp;and witness how Pera-Swarm is redefining the boundaries of innovation.</p>
<p>For collaboration or academic inquiries, please reach out to the project team during the exhibition days.</p>
